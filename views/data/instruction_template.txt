<h3>Enter the Title of Your HIT - this gives workers an idea of what the task is</h3>

<div class="highlight-box">
<p>Enter instructions for your HIT.&nbsp; It&#39;s a good idea to:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Be sure to be as specific as possible in your instructions so that there&#39;s no confusion.&nbsp; For example, when asking workers to extract text from an image, ask workers to type the text exactly as shown in the image including capitalizations, spaces and punctuations.</li>
	<li>Include an example of a right answer, and a wrong answer</li>
	<li>Clarify what you expect if the HIT is not doable because of missing data or other problems</li>
</ul>
</div>
